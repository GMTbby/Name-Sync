/b/ Name Sync

Shares your name with other posters on /b/. Also allows you to assign names to Anonymous posters.

Install Now

Before Installing

  1. Close any /b/ threads or pages
  2. Uninstall old script
  3. Clear boards.4chan.org cookies

Posting

  • Your name, subject and email fields in the QR box are shared (or as specified in options)
  • Only posts with images are shared

Known Issues

  • Tripcodes rarely give correct result

FAQ

I don't use 4chan X, will this script work?
You may be able to see other names. But you will not be able to share your own. Get 4chan X.

What browsers are supported?

  • Chrome/Chromium
  • Firefox/Nightly with Scriptish/Greasemonkey
  • Opera Next
Scriptify, Opera 11 and Safari are unsupported.
